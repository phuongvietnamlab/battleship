Play Battleship Online — Free, in Your Browser

Battleship Online (also called Sea Battle, or cờ hải chiến / bắn tàu in Vietnamese) is a free online version of the classic naval guessing game. Play 2-player with a friend using a room code, or play single-player against the computer bot. No download, no sign-up — it works on desktop and mobile.

How to play Battleship

  • Place your fleet of 5 ships on the 11×11 grid — drag to move, double-tap to rotate, or shuffle randomly.
  • Take turns firing at the enemy waters. A hit lets you fire again; a miss passes the turn.
  • Each turn has a 20-second timer — stall too long and you forfeit.
  • Sink all five enemy ships (Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine, Destroyer) to win.

Game modes

Classic — pure Battleship, no extras. Advance — power-ups appear on the enemy sea; hit them to collect, then use them on your turn (scatter blast, cross missile, extra turn, reveal, sea mine).

Frequently asked questions

Is Battleship online free to play?
Yes. Battleship Online (Sea Battle) is 100% free — no download and no sign-up. It runs in your browser on desktop and mobile.
Can I play Battleship online with a friend?
Yes. Create a room, share the 5-letter room code, and your friend joins to start a real-time 2-player match.
Can I play Battleship against the computer?
Yes. Choose Play vs Bot for a single-player game against the computer — no opponent needed.
Cờ hải chiến (bắn tàu) chơi online miễn phí được không?
Được. Game bắn tàu / cờ hải chiến chơi miễn phí ngay trên trình duyệt, không cần tải hay đăng ký. Tạo phòng gửi mã cho bạn, hoặc đấu với máy.
