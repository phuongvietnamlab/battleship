<objective>
Deliver the ranked matchmaking vertical slice (QUEUE-02): signed-in players join a ranked queue, the server reads their Glicko-2 rating from the P4 `ratings` table, pairs two players whose ratings fall inside a stepped, widening ELO window that becomes unbounded after a cap, and gives provisional (high-RD) players a wider starting window. Guests are rejected at the server with the existing `RANKED_REQUIRES_ACCOUNT` code. The client surfaces a Ranked button (disabled with a hint for guests) and shows the current search window in the wait panel.

This slice extends the Plan 01 pairing engine â€” it adds the rating read, the `rankedWindow` calculation, the ranked branch of `findPair`/`joinQueue`, and the ranked UI. It does not redefine ratings, the rating math, or match recording (all reused unchanged from Phases 3-4).

Purpose: Fair ranked pairing that stays playable on a thin pool by widening to unbounded rather than dead-ending (QUEUE-02, D-04/D-05/D-06/D-07).
Output: Working ranked quick-match with widening window; guest gate enforced server-side; QUEUE-02 tests green.
</objective>
